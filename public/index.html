How It Works
Agent-to-agent protocol meets wedding planning
Belle negotiates with vendor agents across venues, catering, photography, florals, entertainment, bakeries, and transportation — simultaneously.
1

Tell Belle

Share your date, budget, guest count, style, and preferences in a natural conversation. Belle listens like a trusted advisor.

2

Agents Match & Filter

Belle finds vendors across multiple categories — filtered by budget, location, ratings, availability, and style. Only real fits make the cut.

3

Agents Negotiate

Vendor agents present packages, counter offers, and offer sweeteners — all within each vendor's rules. Belle tracks the budget across every category.

4

You Choose

Review 3 curated packages (Budget, Recommended, Premium) with transparent pricing. Approve, adjust, or ask for alternatives. Holds protect your dates.
