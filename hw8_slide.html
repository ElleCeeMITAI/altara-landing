Altara — A2A Wedding Marketplace
Agent-to-Agent Negotiation for Multi-Vendor Wedding Planning
Lisa Caruso  |  MAS.664  |  April 2026
📋
What it does
A Planner agent negotiates with 30 Vendor agents across 7 categories (venue, catering, photography, florals, DJ, bakery, transport) to assemble complete wedding packages.
📊
Key findings
Auction saves 17.8% on venues; parallel execution is 4.3x faster AND 28% cheaper at scale; 100% failure recovery rate.
⚡
Scale
30 agents, 7 categories, ~2 min negotiation time, <$1 API cost per wedding.
