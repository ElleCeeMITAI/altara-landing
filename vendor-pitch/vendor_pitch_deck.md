## Slide 7: Your Reputation Works For You

### Ratings drive bookings. We make yours count.

**Three-layer rating system:**

1. **Platform ratings** — verified reviews from couples who booked through The Big Day (weighted highest)
2. **External ratings** — aggregated from Google, Yelp, The Knot, WeddingWire
3. **Composite score** — blended score that couples see when reviewing packages

**Your agent uses your ratings in negotiations:**
- High ratings justify premium pricing
- Review highlights are quoted to build trust
- "Tried & True Venue Partner" badges show you know the space

**The flywheel:** More bookings through the platform = more verified reviews = higher composite score = more matches = more bookings.

## Appendix: Category-Specific Value Props

### For Venues:
- In-house catering? Your agent automatically bundles it — couples see the convenience advantage
- Your agent mentions your catering policy upfront, saving you the "do you allow outside caterers?" email chain
- Hold management prevents double-booking

### For Caterers:
- Tasting invitations are automated after initial agreement
- Dietary accommodations are communicated upfront (no surprises at the tasting)
- Your agent highlights your bar packages as upsell opportunities

### For Photographers:
- Your agent upsells engagement sessions, second shooters, and albums
- Videography add-ons and drone footage are presented as seamless bundles
- Your retainer model and portfolio are front and center

### For Florists:
- Your agent matches floral scope to the couple's needs (just bouquets vs. full florals)
- Seasonal flower swap suggestions are built into your negotiation tactics
- Eco-friendly practices are highlighted when couples care about sustainability

### For DJs/Bands:
- Genre matching ensures you only get inquiries from couples who want your style
- Playlist consultation process is presented as a selling point
- Venue partnerships show you know the sound setup and logistics

### For Bakeries:
- Tasting process is built into the booking flow
- Dessert alternatives (cupcake towers, donut walls) are presented alongside cakes
- Dietary accommodations are matched before you even hear from the couple

### For Transportation:
- Vehicle viewing invitations are automated
- Eco-friendly options (electric/hybrid) are highlighted for couples who care
- Multi-vehicle discounts are offered automatically for bridal party bookings
