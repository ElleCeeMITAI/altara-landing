# The Big Day — Vendor Partner One-Pager

## Your AI Agent Sells While You Create.

The Big Day is an AI-powered wedding marketplace launching in Boston. Your personalized vendor agent negotiates with couples' planner agents on your behalf — 24/7, simultaneously, at scale. You set your pricing and style once. Your agent handles unlimited inquiries. You only hear about it when there's a real booking ready to confirm.

---

### How It Works

1. **You set up your agent** (30 min) — pricing, packages, negotiation style, service area
2. **Couples tell their AI planner** what they want — budget, date, style, preferences
3. **Your agent is matched** to couples who fit your business — budget, location, style, availability
4. **Your agent negotiates** within your parameters — presents packages, counters offers, offers sweeteners
5. **You confirm the booking** — review the terms, accept, collect deposit. Done.
